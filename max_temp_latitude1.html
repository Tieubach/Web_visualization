Summary: Latitude vs. X

This is a correlation plot of city location by latitude and the max temperature collected. The max temperature was highest between -20 to +20 degrees Latitude. Intersetingly, it seems the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer compare to northern hemisphere for city location further away from equator such as above/ below +- 40 degree of latitude.
